About This Project

Privacy-First Disease Monitoring

This application processes all data locally in your browser, ensuring your medical information never leaves your device. We use advanced AI models to analyze photos and medical reports for educational and research purposes.

How It Works

  • Photo Analysis: Upload images of symptoms for AI-powered classification
  • Report Processing: Analyze medical reports for disease patterns
  • Local Processing: All analysis happens on your device
  • Secure Storage: Data encrypted and stored locally

Data Sources

We use synthetic data and properly licensed public datasets to train our models. No real patient data is used in training or analysis.
